Unit Details

Caustic Dissolving Unit

The capacity of this unit is 6 tons of solid caustic in 6 working hours. The feed of this unit is the solid caustic or %50 liquid caustic soda. The required amount of caustic soda of the refinery units is prepared in this plant with the specified concentration. The products of this unit are %40 strong caustic, %18.7 diluted caustic and %2.8 weak caustic soda.
